Civil Service People Survey Results Explorer

This microsite provides interactive tools to explore the published results of the Civil Service People Survey results. Please note that while this site is based on published data, this is an experimental prototype and a work in progress, as such is not an official publication of the Cabinet Office and does not represent government policy. For full details and results please visit the Civil Service People Survey Hub on GOV.UK.
